At a glance — three pillars

Fine-grained access & sharing

Move beyond project-level RBAC: single-asset control and sharing (Plastic-backed AM 2.0), staging for key automotive customers, then VPC migration and public cloud GA.

VPC, on-prem & security

Deployment simplification (VPCTL), admin parity with public cloud, faster public/VPC parity, SOC 2 Type II, Studio on VPC — aligned to how Industry pipeline actually sells.

Pipeline automation & AT

UPA queuing and license behavior on VPC, migration of built-in workflows, consumption SKU, licensing integration, and continued format support (e.g. HOOPS).
